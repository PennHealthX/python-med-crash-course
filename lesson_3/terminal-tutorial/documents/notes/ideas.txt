Research Project Ideas - Spring 2024

1. Predicting sepsis onset using vital signs time series
   - Use ICU monitoring data (HR, BP, temp, lactate)
   - Early warning system for clinical decision support

2. Natural language processing of radiology reports
   - Extract structured findings from unstructured text
   - Identify incidental findings for follow-up

3. Analyzing social determinants of health in diabetes outcomes
   - Link census data with patient outcomes
   - Identify high-risk communities for intervention

4. Machine learning for drug-drug interaction prediction
   - Use medication history and adverse event reports
   - Clinical decision support tool for prescribers
